The Style Edit

A year-round digital directory

Every featured business supports Gunter Football, and every purchase with a Blitz & Glitz code gives back — the fundraiser keeps running for weeks after the event.

Before the event

Businesses join the Edit

Website feature, logo & spotlight, direct link, 3–5 featured products, an exclusive discount code, and social mentions leading up to August 23rd.

During the event

Guests scan & browse

QR codes on every table, in every swag bag, and on event signage. Guests browse and shop their favorites all evening long.

After the event

Codes keep giving back

Each business shares a custom code that discounts for shoppers, tracks sales, and donates a percentage back to Gunter Football.
