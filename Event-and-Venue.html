Theme & Look

Soft, watercolor & glam.

Butter yellow and blush florals with champagne table runners, set against soft greens. Vintage gold and a touch of silver on signage, balloons, and tableware. It plays beautifully against the venue’s white-washed wood and gold chiavari chairs.

Name play — “Blitz” (football) + “Glitz” (glamour). Keep the glam front and center and the football reference light. This is a night for moms to feel celebrated.

Venue notes

Naturally elegant — minimal decor needed. The space does the heavy lifting.

IWhite-washed barn-style hall with vaulted wood-beam ceilings and crystal chandeliers.
IIGold chiavari chairs and round tables with white linens already in place — perfect for our palette.
IIILarge windows, abundant natural light, and polished concrete floors.
IVA loft / upper level overlooking the main hall — great for a photo moment or VIP area.
VBuilt-in console/bar-height surface along one wall; farmhouse table tucked under the staircase.
VIOpen floor plan that flexes easily between seated dinner, vendor booths, and mingling.

Across the evening

Activities & components

What’s happening over the three-hour night.

Dinner & drinks
Camillo’s (Gunter, TX) is donating Mexican food with appetizers, water, and tea.
Bar (venue rules)
Must use One Preston’s bartenders. Beer, wine & champagne only — no liquor. Both specialty drinks must be wine-based.
Swag bags
Canvas yacht-style tote with a custom GT patch for every attendee, filled with vendor samples & sponsor goodies.
Door prizes
Drawn throughout the night to keep energy high.
Local vendor booths
Boutiques, beauty, home goods, and more — booth fee structure TBD.
Raffles
Raffle baskets & prizes with ticket sales — run through GiveSmart.
