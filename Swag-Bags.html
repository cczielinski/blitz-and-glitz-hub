The Curated Ten

Items sponsors can brand

Upgrade Your Brand Exposure

Sponsorship levels include a complimentary or optional Tier 1 item. Upgrade to Tier 2 or Tier 3 for an additional cost — bigger, more-used items that keep your brand visible longer.

Tier 1

Everyday giveaway items that make a great first impression.

Tier 2

Practical, reusable items—like water bottles and other premium accessories—that receive frequent use and increased brand exposure.

Tier 3

High-value signature items—such as stadium blankets and other event favorites—that offer maximum visibility and long-term use.

Tier 1

01
Can koozies / drink sleeves
Low cost, high keep rate, full-color logo. Everyone uses them.
02
Lip balm or mini sunscreen
Mom-friendly, branded label, fits perfectly in any bag.
03
Pom-poms / spirit shakers
Fun, photogenic, and school-color hype in every photo.
04
Rally towels / spirit towels
Classic football giveaway; big print area; used at every game.
05
Folding hand fans / stadium fans
Beat the Texas heat; full-color print surface. Practical and on-brand.

Tier 2

01

Daily-use keepsake; keeps drinks cold; wrap-around branding.

Meets stadium clear-bag policies; practical & stylish; great logo placement.
03
Stadium seat cushions
High value, used every home game — your logo seen all season long.

Tier 3

01

Premium feel for cool fall nights; embroidered or printed logo.

Printed game-day ponchos
